Quick Tips

Choose the right OCR engine
  • Gemini: Best for complex layouts & AI-assisted understanding (95%+ accuracy)
  • Tesseract: Fast processing for standard printed text (85-90% accuracy)
  • Paddle: Excellent for handwritten text & multilingual content
  • Qwen-2.5-VL-32B: Best for structured documents with visual understanding
  • Surya: Layout-aware extraction for complex document structures (92%+ accuracy)
